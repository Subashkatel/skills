# Research review checklist

## Evidence extraction
- Citation and version/date.
- Problem statement.
- Assumptions.
- Definitions and notation.
- Algorithm/protocol.
- Complexity and resource claims.
- Experiments and baselines.
- Limitations.

## Implementation extraction
- Inputs and outputs.
- Data structures.
- Pseudocode.
- Parameters and defaults.
- Validation tests.
- Reproduction commands.
- Dependencies.

## Confidence labels
- Directly stated by source.
- Derived from source.
- Inferred by reviewer.
- Speculative or needs validation.
