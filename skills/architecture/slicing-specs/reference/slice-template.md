# Scientific Slice Template

## Contract
What this slice proves or unlocks.

## Seam and ownership
Module, API, kernel, IR pass, decoder interface, data schema, or experiment boundary.

## Inputs and fixtures
Required datasets, seeds, circuits, noise model, hardware profile, benchmark size, or reference artifact.

## Verification gates
Unit tests, property tests, profiler run, numerical comparison, QEC simulation, logical-error estimate, or architecture review.

## Evidence to record
Commands, outputs, profiler summaries, tolerances, confidence intervals, and known limitations.

## Scope firewall
What this slice must not change.

## Removal condition
Temporary scaffolding, if any, and the slice that removes it.
